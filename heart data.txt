Project 2 heart.csv Dataset for DS110

1. age - age in years

2. sex - sex (1 = male; 0 = female)

3. cp - chest pain type (0 = asymptomatic; 1 = non-anginal pain; 2 = typical angina; 3 = severe angina;)

4. trestbps - resting blood pressure (in mm Hg on admission to the hospital)

5. chol - serum cholestoral in mg/dl

6. fbs - fasting blood sugar mg/dL (below 125 is considered healthy)

7. restecg - resting electrocardiographic results (0 = normal; 1 = hypertrophy)

8. thalach - maximum heart rate achieved

9. exang - exercise induced angina (; 0 = no; 1 = yes)

10. oldpeak - ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest

11. output - diagnosis of heart disease (angiographic disease status) (0 = No; Value 1 = Yes)
